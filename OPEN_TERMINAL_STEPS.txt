HOW TO OPEN TERMINAL IN CURSOR - STEP BY STEP
==============================================

METHOD 1: Using the Menu (Easiest)
-----------------------------------
1. Look at the TOP of your Cursor window
2. Click on the word "View" in the menu bar
3. In the dropdown menu, click "Terminal" or "Integrated Terminal"
4. A panel will appear at the BOTTOM of your window
5. That's your terminal!

METHOD 2: Keyboard Shortcut
----------------------------
1. Press: Control + ` (backtick)
   - The backtick key (`) is above the Tab key
   - On some keyboards, it's left of the number 1 key
2. The terminal will open at the bottom

METHOD 3: Command Palette
--------------------------
1. Press: Cmd + Shift + P (on Mac) or Ctrl + Shift + P (Windows)
2. Type: "terminal"
3. Select: "Terminal: Create New Terminal"
4. Terminal opens at the bottom

WHERE TO LOOK:
--------------
- The terminal opens at the VERY BOTTOM of your Cursor window
- It's below your code editor
- It might be collapsed - look for a thin gray bar at the bottom
- Click that bar to expand the terminal

WHAT YOU'LL SEE:
----------------
Once open, you'll see something like:
  zeshaanqureshi@MacBookPro Nomad-Stop-NextJS %

Then you can type commands!
